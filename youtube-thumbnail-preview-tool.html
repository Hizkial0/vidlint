Preview and Analyze

YouTube thumbnail preview tool plus diagnosis

A plain preview tool shows how your image might look in the feed. VidLint goes a step further. It helps you preview the thumbnail and understand why it may underperform, so you can make a better pre-publish choice instead of guessing from appearance alone.

Preview is useful for

  • Checking whether the image is too dark at small size.
  • Seeing if text becomes unreadable on mobile.
  • Comparing two visual directions quickly.

Diagnosis is what closes the gap

If a thumbnail looks weak, you still need to know whether the issue is clutter, misplaced text, soft emotion, or a missing focal point. That is the difference between a preview tool and a thumbnail analyzer.
