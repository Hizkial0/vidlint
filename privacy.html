Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use VidLint, we collect:

  • Account info: Your name, email, and profile picture from Google Sign-In.
  • Uploaded images: Thumbnail images you upload for analysis. These are processed and not shared with third parties.
  • Usage data: Basic analytics like page views and feature usage to improve the product.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze your thumbnails and provide scores and feedback.
  • To manage your account and subscription.
  • To improve VidLint's accuracy and features.
  • To communicate with you about your account (only when necessary).

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Uploaded thumbnails are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored unless you save them to your account history.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate VidLint:

  • Google OAuth: For authentication.
  • Supabase: For user management and database.
  • OpenAI / Google Gemini: For AI-powered analysis (your images are sent to these services for processing).

5. Your Rights

You can request to delete your account and all associated data at any time by emailing zeke@vidlint.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on the site.

8. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us: zeke@vidlint.com
