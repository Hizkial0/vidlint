Analyze My Thumbnail

Gaming thumbnail analyzer built for fast CTR reads

Gaming thumbnails behave differently from generic business or lifestyle thumbnails. They often rely on high contrast, bold emotion, chaos control, and a very clear hero moment. VidLint is a gaming thumbnail analyzer that checks whether all of that still works at mobile size.

What gaming creators need

  • A readable hero at first glance.
  • One clear moment instead of noisy scene coverage.
  • Contrast that survives dark game worlds and crowded UIs.

Common gaming failures

Too many subjects, tiny weapons or bosses, overbuilt text, and thumbnails that explain lore but never sell the click. Those are exactly the cases where a gaming-first analyzer helps.
