YouTube Thumbnail Tester for fast readability checks

VidLint works as a YouTube thumbnail tester for creators who want a quick answer before they publish. You upload the image, choose the game context, and get direct feedback on whether the thumbnail reads fast enough to earn the click.

What a strong tester should show you

  1. Whether the hero is still readable at small sizes.
  2. Whether text helps the promise or steals attention.
  3. Whether the thumbnail sells one clear moment instead of several weak ones.

How to use VidLint as a tester

Run your draft through the upload flow, compare the score with the suggested fixes, then use the examples page to sanity check whether your concept is too broad, too cluttered, or too soft on mobile.
