YouTube Thumbnail Analyzer for pre-publish fixes

VidLint is a YouTube thumbnail analyzer built for creators who want more than a preview. Upload a draft, see what steals attention, and get direct fixes for hierarchy, readability, and mobile click risk before the thumbnail ever hits the feed.

What the analyzer checks

  • Whether the main subject stays readable at mobile size.
  • Whether text or background clutter steals first glance.
  • Whether the image sells one clear click-worthy moment.

Why creators use it

Most thumbnails fail before the video has a chance. A fast analyzer helps you catch weak contrast, confused focus, and soft hooks while the image is still easy to fix.

Why VidLint is different

VidLint is not just a preview tool. It explains what to change, which subject to promote, and why the current packaging may lose clicks.

Best next step

If you already have a draft, run it through the tester and compare the result with our examples page. That gives you the fastest path from rough concept to stronger packaging.
