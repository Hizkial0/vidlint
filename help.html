Help Center

Find answers to common questions below. For anything else, email us at zeke@vidlint.com.

Getting Started

How do I check my thumbnail?

Click "Check My Thumbnail" on the homepage, sign in with Google, upload your thumbnail image, select your game, and click analyze. You'll get a score and actionable fixes within seconds.

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG and JPG files up to 5MB. For best results, upload your thumbnail at 1280×720 resolution (standard YouTube thumbnail size).

Do I need a Google account?

Yes. We use Google Sign-In to manage your account and keep your analysis history private. We don't access any of your YouTube data.

Analysis & Results

What does the score mean?

Your score (0–100) reflects how strong your thumbnail is across clarity, contrast, focus, text readability, and emotional impact. Above 70 is solid. Below 50 needs work.

How accurate is the analysis?

VidLint uses computer vision and AI models trained on thousands of thumbnails. It catches the most common weaknesses reliably, but it's a tool — not a guarantee. Use the fixes as strong suggestions.

Can I analyze drafts or unfinished thumbnails?

Yes! That's exactly what VidLint is designed for. Upload your rough draft, see what's weak, and fix it before you publish.
