About VidLint

VidLint helps YouTube creators catch weak thumbnails before they go live. Upload your thumbnail, get a fast score, and see exactly what to fix — before you burn impressions.

Why We Built This

Most creators spend hours making videos but only minutes on thumbnails. Yet the thumbnail is the single biggest factor in whether someone clicks. We built VidLint to give every creator a fast, honest check before they hit publish.

How It Works

VidLint uses computer vision and AI to analyze your thumbnail for clarity, contrast, focus, text readability, and emotional impact. You get a score, a list of specific fixes, and stronger visual directions — all in seconds.

Who We Are

VidLint is built by a small team obsessed with thumbnails, click-through rates, and helping creators grow. We're based on the idea that better thumbnails mean better channels.
